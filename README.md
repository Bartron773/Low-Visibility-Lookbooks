# Low Visibility — Lookbooks

A digital archive of **Low Visibility** fashion concepts, campaigns, silhouettes, materials, and collection studies developed by **Bart Salazar / Simple Minds Studios**.

## About the Archive

This repository collects visual lookbooks and related presentation experiments within the Low Visibility creative universe. It is intended to preserve the evolution of the work while presenting each collection as a designed editorial artifact rather than a loose image dump.

## Live Project

[View the Low Visibility Lookbooks](https://bartron773.github.io/Low-Visibility-Lookbooks/)

## Scope

The archive may include:

- collection lookbooks and campaign imagery
- garment, swim, and accessory concepts
- visual identity and palette studies
- AI-assisted image-development workflows
- editorial layouts and presentation systems

## Status

Active creative archive. Some pieces are speculative, conceptual, or AI-assisted and are not manufacturing specifications or commercially available products unless expressly stated.
